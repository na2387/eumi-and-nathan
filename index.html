3 TINY LOVE STORIES

ON FIRST DATES

When Eumi agreed to a date, Nathan rushed to his local coffee shop and asked the other regulars for recommendations. The consensus was the High Line. Eumi was impressed when Nathan didn’t suggest the typical Tinder date at a bar. On the date, Nathan sweetly shared that he’d had help in brainstorming; Eumi, charmed by his candor, shared that she was wearing her lucky skirt.


ON BEING EACH OTHER'S BETTER HALF

Over the years Nathan and Eumi have each been slipping the other the better half: the cheesier slice of pizza, the fuller glass of juice. One night a truce was called. They both pretended not to want the same cookie so that the other could eat it. The stalemate became literal when neither ate it and the cookie went stale. Learning from this, they agreed to be honest from then on (though they’re absolutely still sneaking the other the better half).


ON TO NEW ADVENTURES

Big changes are happening. Nathan is starting a new career; Eumi is continuing to explore her creativity. Nathan is moving to Philadelphia; Eumi is moving to Queens. Despite the distance, we are looking forward to coming back together and celebrating our wedding with all of you.
